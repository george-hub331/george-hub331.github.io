Hi, welcome to my website! :)

I am a Software Engineer and Blockchain Researcher with Hezron Labs, where I work on the design and implementation of blockchain protocols. Currently, I'm working on building Cryptea; a Wallet-as-a-Service application for businesses and individuals.

My interests include Core blockchain engineering, Chain Abstraction, and Scalability of blockchain protocols, with my primary aim being to drive blockchain user adoption by abstracting the difficulties of interaction.

Recent Projects

  • Cryptea: A product that enables users accept crypto as a payment method, and provide Web3 services to their users, using a Wallet-as-a-Service infrastructure.

  • FilCRM: A decentralized CRM application for businesses, which assists businesses with lead management, and monitoring. Fully decentralised with total storage on Filecoin through Lighthouse, aimed to eliminate user security issues.

  • Spect: Spect provides web3 forms, projects & automations to coordinate & build DAOs the decentralized way. Funded by PolygonDAO, GR14 & Push Protocol

  • Clover: A DAO framework for projects built on the Filecoin Virtual Machine. Includes a governance system for projects, a chat interface for users, decentralised storage for DAOs, and a token-gated system for security.

  • Promise Protocol: A product that helps DAOs reward early contributors with future equity backed by collateral.
