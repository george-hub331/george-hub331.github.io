Hi there, welcome to my website!

I’m a Software Engineer with 8 years of experience building high-performance web and blockchain applications. My core tech stack includes React.js, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js, PHP, and Laravel. I also develop decentralized applications using Ethers.js, Web3.js, and Solidity.

I specialize in full-stack development—creating responsive frontends, designing scalable backend APIs, and deploying serverless functions. With a strong foundation in database architecture and Linux server management, I deliver end-to-end solutions that are secure, efficient, and production-ready.

I’m passionate about emerging technologies, particularly in blockchain and decentralized systems. I enjoy building tools and platforms that push technical boundaries, and I’m always looking to collaborate on projects that make a real impact.
